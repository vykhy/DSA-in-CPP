Conventions
Best case - Omega
Worst case - Big oh
Avg case - - sign

Cube > Square > Linear > Square root > Logn > constant


1. Linear 
    - O(n)
    - directly proportional to length of input

2. Quadratic
    - O(n * n)
    - squared proportion to length of input

3. Constant
    - O(1)
    - constant time/space. Not dependant on input.
